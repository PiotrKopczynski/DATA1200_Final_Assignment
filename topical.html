Technology and sustainability

Reflection task from module 5:

Watch the video below.

Technological innovations can improve the lives of disadvantaged people in many ways. At the same time, the consumption of electronic devices has an environmental cost.

What are your thoughts about this dilemma?

Feasable:

  1. Instead of using technological innovations, more people could be hired to help disadvantaged people to function normally.
  2. Develop technological aids using reusable materials.
  3. People need to learn how to properly care for their devices so they can last longer.
  4. Genetically engineer humans so they do not get born with disabilities(CRISPR).
  5. Develop more secure cities such that less people become disadvantaged during their lifetime.
  6. Apply spartan law that disregards people with disadvantages.
  7. Next generation users of electronic devices need to be educated in sustainable use of “use and throw” articles like a mobile phone.
  8. Producers of electronic devices have to engage in the full life cycle of their products, from production to use and recycling of scarce materials.
  9. Apple needs to stop producing new Iphones so people will stop buying new phones every half year.
  10. A possible way of viewing the issue is that when disadvantaged people receive technological aids, they have the potential to become functional members of society that contribute to saving the environment through innovation.

Far fetched:

  1. People with disabilities should have first priority in the use of electronic devices since it enhances their life value. Fully able people don't need the devices as “life depending” but “nice to have” and entertainment.
  2. Nuke the whole human population so the earth can rebuild itself without us.
  3. Destroy all technology and go back to the bronze age.
  4. Disadvantaged people are not worth the environmental cost so the technological aids should not be developed and made.
  5. Hire disadvantaged people to clean the plastic out of the seas and plant trees to compensate for the pollution due to their aids.
  6. We should launch all non-recyclable devices with a rocket into the sun
  7. Develop cures for different cases of disabilities.
  8. Invest more into sustainability such that we can afford the pollution created by the electronic devices.
  9. Take money from the millionaires and billionaires and divide it between all people.
  10. Make a Cure machine which can cure every disease and disorder.
